# 2 day PSPO versus 8 week PSPO

#shorts #shortsvideo #shortvideo The PSPO or Professional Scrum Product Owner course from Scrum.Org is the perfect way to acquire and validate the knowledge, skills, and capability to become a #productowner in a #scrumteam. 

Traditionally, the course has been presented over 2 full days, but #scrumorg have launched an 8-week immersive learning experience and that promises to be a game-changer. In this short video, Martin Hinshelwood explains the difference between the two.
