# In what circumstances is agile consulting appropriate?

The transition from traditional #projectmanagement to #agile #productdevelopment can be tough. The cocreators of #scrum have openly stated that it is easy to understand but incredibly difficult to master.

So, under what circumstances can an #agileconsultant have a significant impact on your team's collaborative capability and creative output? When is it beneficial to bring an #agileconsultant into the environment to help guide and mentor your #agile team?

In this short video, Martin Hinshelwood walks us through the appropriate circumstances to invest in an #agileconsultant and how that will empower your team to successfully adopt #scrum.
