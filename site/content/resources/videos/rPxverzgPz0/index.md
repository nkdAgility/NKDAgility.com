# Would you recommend the APS course to a newbie scrum team?

You've decided to adopt #scrum and everyone on the #scrumteam is chomping at the bit to create heaps of value and delight customers something fierce.

Great. Maybe it's not going to plan. Maybe you're finding it tougher to work in an #agile way and bring all the pieces of #scrum together effectively.

What do you do? How do you move out of the quicksand and onto firm ground? In this short video, Martin Hinshwelwood talks about the #APS or Applying Professional Scrum course from scrum.org and how it can help you align with value creation effectively.
