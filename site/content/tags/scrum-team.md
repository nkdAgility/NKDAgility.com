  **Use this category only for discussions on Scrum Team.**  
  The purpose of this category is to explore the collaboration, roles, and dynamics that contribute to the effectiveness of a high-performing Scrum Team. It focuses on how team members interact, communicate, and work together to deliver value in an Agile environment.

  **Key topics to be discussed under this category include:**
  - Roles and responsibilities within a Scrum Team (Scrum Master, Product Owner, Development Team)
  - Team collaboration techniques and practices
  - Communication strategies and tools for Scrum Teams
  - The importance of trust and psychological safety in team dynamics
  - Techniques for fostering a self-organising team
  - Conflict resolution and decision-making processes within the team
  - Continuous improvement practices (e.g., retrospectives)
  - The impact of team composition and diversity on performance
  - Metrics and indicators of team performance and health

  **Strictly exclude** unrelated content such as general Agile principles not specific to Scrum, discussions on non-Scrum methodologies, or any misinterpretations of team dynamics that do not align with the Scrum framework as outlined in the Scrum Guide and by key figures like Ken Schwaber and Mike Beedle.
