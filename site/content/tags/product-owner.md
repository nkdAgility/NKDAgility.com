  - Stakeholder engagement and communication

  - Collaboration with development teams to ensure clarity and alignment
  - Metrics and tools for measuring product value and success
  - The role of the Product Owner in Agile ceremonies (e.g., sprint planning, reviews)
  - Challenges faced by Product Owners and strategies to overcome them
