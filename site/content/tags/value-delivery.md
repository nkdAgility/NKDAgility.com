Instructions: |-
  **Use this category only for discussions on Value Delivery.**  
  The scope of this category encompasses the principles and practices aimed at maximising the flow of value from ideation to deployment while minimising waste. It focuses on ensuring that the delivery process is efficient, effective, and aligned with customer needs.

  **Key topics that should be discussed under this category include:**
  - The principles of Lean thinking and its application in value delivery.
  - Techniques for identifying and eliminating waste in processes.
  - The role of Agile methodologies in enhancing value delivery.
  - Strategies for optimising flow in Kanban systems.
  - The importance of continuous feedback loops in delivering value.
  - Metrics and KPIs for measuring value delivery effectiveness.
  - The integration of DevOps practices to streamline deployment and enhance value.
  - Evidence-Based Management approaches to assess and improve value delivery.

  **Strictly exclude** discussions that deviate from the core focus on value delivery, such as unrelated technical implementations, personal opinions on Agile practices without reference to established theories, or content that does not address the flow of value in a systematic manner.
